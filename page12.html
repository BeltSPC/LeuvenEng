Environment Friendly

Environment Friendly: Our belt is mostly manufactured with materials that would otherwise end up on the garbage dump. We give old seatbelts a second chance and transform them into a completely new product.

Stylish

Stylish: Our product is unisex, modern and fashionable. We have a range of buckles in various colours and finishes to suit all tastes.

Comfort

Comfort: The buckle is user-friendly. You can adjust the tightness of the belt any time. The belt has a soft touch and a nice finish which makes it easy to use without damage to your clothing.

Multifunctional

Multifunctional: Our buckle fits any similar type of belts. You can also use the buckle as a bottle opener. We strongly recommend to only use the buckle for this purpose under proper supervision.  

Ethical Enterprise

Belt is an ethical enterprise; we want to raise awareness about road safety. We hope that by wearing a recycled seatbelt our customers will realize the importance of wearing a seatbelt in the car.
